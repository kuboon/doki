doki is another internet time concept
current time is

doki

Concept

doki is designed for easy scheduling over countries.

doki is always UTC. Same doki means same time all over the world.

10 doki is an hour.

1 doki is 6 minutes.

:1 doki is 1 minute.

The first letter indicates week in UTC.

MMonday
TTuesday
WWednesday
RthuRsday
FFriday
SSatday
UsUnday

Examples

08:00 (+00:00) TuesdayT080 doki
09:30 (+00:00) Sunday D095 doki
23:59 (+00:00) SaturdayS239:5 doki
08:12 (+01:00) ThursdayR072 doki
08:00 (+09:00) TuesdayM230 doki
